Core Beliefs

Cloud Architecture Principles.

These principles are at the core of our business. In addition to the AWS Well architected Framework, we use these principles to guide our solutions.

@@include("assets/img/icons/duotone-icons/General/Scale.svg")

Elastic by Nature

Technology should stretch to meet the needs of your business, this stretching should not cause permanent change (damage) to your technology.

@@include("assets/img/icons/duotone-icons/Shopping/Chart-Bar#1.svg")

Resilient in Design

We should design our technology with the reality of failures in mind, creating different failure depths where functionality is reduced in favor of a total failure.

@@include("assets/img/icons/duotone-icons/Tools/Hummer.svg")

Performance on Demand.

Technology should do what it is told, when it is told, it should be ready to respond to changing environments in technology and in your business.
